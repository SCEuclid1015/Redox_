About

Purpose: Explains the aim of the project, which is to create an interactive humanoid robot. Technology Used: Mentions the ESP32-CAM and the advanced technologies employed, such as computer vision and machine learning. Functionality: Highlights the robot’s capabilities, such as object recognition and autonomous navigation. Goal: Conveys the broader objective of exploring robotics and creating a user-friendly interactive experience.

Features

Gesture Control: The robot can be controlled using hand gestures, providing an intuitive way to interact. Voice Command: It responds to user voice commands for natural interaction. Autonomous Navigation: The robot can navigate its environment independently. Remote Monitoring: It streams live video feeds for remote observation. Learning Algorithms: The robot adapts and improves its performance over time.

Components

ESP32-CAM: The microcontroller that serves as the brain of the robot, equipped with a camera for image processing and control. Servo Driver: Manages the movement of the servo motors, allowing for precise control of the robot's movements. Servo Motors: Actuate the robot’s limbs and joints, enabling movement and articulation. Jumper Wires: Used for connecting various components together for communication and power. Fibre Body: Provides a lightweight and durable structure for the robot. Lithium Battery: Powers the robot, offering a reliable and efficient energy source.
